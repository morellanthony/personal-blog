Review

A great certification. I would highly recommend the training and exam. However, people that have mobile testing experience may not find this exam challenging at all.

The Training

The training for this exam was on par with the exam. There should be nothing of surprise. I enjoyed the content of the training as it helped introduce me to the world of hacking mobile applications.

The Exam

This exam was easy. Probably the easiest one I have done. Aaron, the course and exam creator has stated that the only android portion is needed to pass the exam, I can confirm that. The time to take this exam and write the report is plenty.

Overall

This exam and training does a great job comfortably introducing beginners into mobile applications. This exam took me a total of 9 days, training and exam. It may help a little bit to understand basic Burpsuite functions and very basic web application vulnerabilities, but users may be fine even without that.
