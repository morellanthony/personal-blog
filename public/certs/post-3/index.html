Posted on May 21, 2023

eWPT by eLearnSecurity

​​I am eWPT certified! ​

Review

Over the past few months, I have been able to increase my understanding of web security while wrapping up my master’s in cybersecurity. I wanted to find something that would train me while not going over my workload while in school and working, the eWPT felt fine for that.

The Training

The training felt like it ran in parallel with the testing material. I thought the training material was basic but I like to make sure my fundamentals are strong before I take on more difficult certifications this and next year.

The Exam

The test itself was a bit buggy, which I won’t add any more other than there were some inconsistencies with simulations. This made the testing environment frustrating. I also felt like I had written and documented more than anything throughout this exam. This gave me a new perspective on penetration testing, because it may not always be technical work.

Overall

Unfortunately, because of the exam environment being buggy I cannot recommend this exam. The training felt good, but when compared to HTB CBBH, the content felt much more thorough. I think the eWPT had good training content that reinforced the content learned for the CBBH, however it was never necessary for the CBBH. A big thanks to the TCM penetration testing report template.
